# Valley Day

Welcome to The Fellowship :D 

This is a LOTR inspired Farming / Survival Game.
The base concept is simple, plant crops, watch them grow and farm them once they have matured but before they rot.


The player controls a farmer navigating a grid based farm that is cluttered with debris and surrounded by fences. The main goal is to clear debris plant and harvest crops and ultimately find and unlock the hidden farm gate before the daylight timer runs out.

Each playthrough is based on an external map file allowing different layouts items and challenges without changing the game code.
The game is won when the player harvests the required number of crops uncovers the exit reaches the exit before daylight ends.
The game is lost if the daylight countdown reaches zero or the player has no remaining Health Points (HP).

There are multiple difficulties, the hard one is pretty hard so start with easy or medium first :D 



Controls:
- Arrow keys: Move the player
- A: Plant seeds or harvest crops
- D hold: Remove debris
- S: Hit Wildlife with sword
- Q: use Staff if you have found one 
- Esc: Open game menu and pause gameplay

"Wildlife" (monsters is more accurate though :D):
- every animal/monster can destroy your crops if they walk on it and damage you when they touch you
- animals:
- chicken -> just exists
- bull -> extremly likes the color you wear... When he sees you he's gonna chase you
- monsters:
- balrog -> (in Felix' opinion coolest monster) -> every couple of seconds he will burn many tiles around him and damage you 
- dragon -> (in Franz' opinion coolest monster) -> if you get too close he will burn on you to death. His name is "O Bicho", portuguese for "the Beast".

items:
- watering can -> re-vitilizes all of your rotten crops to a mature stage -> one time effect
- shovel -> makes better at mining than Steve from Minecraft -> 1/2 your debris taking time
- fertilizer -> upgrades all less than mature crops to one stage further
- ring -> One Ring to rule them all, one Ring to find them, one ring to rule them all and in the darkness bind them (you get the effects Frodo gets when he slips it on, if you dont know what that means you probably should watch some good movies) -> time limited effect
- fire resistance potion -> kind of obvious I hope -> time limited effect
- Staff -> lets you defeat one and just one animal and it has more range than your sword so use it wisely


types of ground:
- soil - > plantable and walkable
- path -> you cannot place crops on them -> walkable
- fence -> map border, indestructable -> not walkable
- unbroken debris -> can be broken -> not walkable
- broken debris -> walkable, reveals items below

planting crops:
-  in Order to meet the crop quota and unlock the exit, you must plant and harvest crops.
- Press a while looking at a soil tile, to plant a seed and dont forget to harvest it in the same way, once it has matured
- careful though, dont let your crops be eaten or rott. If left unatended Crops will rott within 55-65 seconds, however if they do, you can allways use the watering can to restore them.
